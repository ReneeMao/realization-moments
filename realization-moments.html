Realization moments

A circular journey through thriving, pressure, loss, and renewal — each stage a vessel shaped by values and glazed by feeling.

1
Thriving
happy · gold glaze self-transcendence · bowl

Goals are met through connection and contribution. The wide bowl holds others as much as itself.

2
Overwhelmed
angry · red glaze self-enhancement · tall vase

Pressure mounts. The tall vase strains upward, driven by personal ambition exceeding its limits.

4
Healing
happy · gold glaze openness to change · teapot

Joy returns — but through a new path. The teapot pours outward, open to transformation and possibility.

3
Breaking
sad · blue glaze self-transcendence · bowl

The same values that held joy now hold grief. The bowl's shape is unchanged — only the feeling within has shifted.

How to read this

Glaze color → emotion category

Happy (gold)
Angry (terracotta red)
Sad (cobalt blue)
Fearful (violet)
Surprised (amber)
Disgusted (teal)

Vessel shape → core value category

Wide bowl · Self-transcendence
Tall vase · Self-enhancement
Squat pot · Conservation
Teapot · Openness to change
Stages 1 & 3 share the same bowl shape — the same values, but grief where joy once was.
Stages 1 & 4 share the same gold glaze — happiness returns, but through an entirely new vessel.

Break, mend, and carry the gold forward.
